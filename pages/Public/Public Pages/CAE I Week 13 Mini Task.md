### Lesson Plan: Festivals and Traditions
**Objective:**  
Students will work in teams to create a presentation comparing and contrasting different festivals and traditions from around the world. Each team member will create one slide discussing a specific festival or tradition.
**Group Formation and Topic Assignment**
- See teams above.
- Assign each team a set of festivals or traditions to research. Ensure a diverse range of festivals from different cultures.
**Research and Slide Creation**
- **Instructions:** Each team member will research one festival or tradition and create a slide. The slide should include:
    - Name of the festival/tradition
    - Country/region of origin
    - Key activities and customs
    - Historical or cultural significance
    - Visuals (images, videos, etc.)
**Team Collaboration**
- Teams will come together to review each memberâ€™s slide.
- Discuss similarities and differences between the festivals/traditions.
- Prepare a brief presentation summarizing their findings.
**Presentations**
- ==Present slides to instructor for feedback (before creating the video).==
    - Karol, Gaby, DianaC, and Ale
    - Hannia & Jorge
- Each team creates a video where each team member presents their slide and talks for three-to-four minutes each.
- Each team should compare and contrast different festivals and/or traditions.
- Each presentation should have an introduction (one slide), body (one slide per person), and conclusion (one slide).
### **Materials Needed:**
