- Review [[Skimming and Scanning]].
    
    ### Tactics for Skimming
    
    1. **Read Titles, Subheadings, and Bold Text**
        
        These elements often summarize the main topics and ideas.
        
    2. **Look at the First and Last Sentences of Paragraphs**
        
        In many English texts, the topic sentence comes first and the concluding sentence sums up the main point.
        
    3. **Notice Visual Cues**
        
        Images, charts, bullet points, and highlighted words can provide quick insight into content.
        
    4. **Avoid Stopping for Unknown Words**
        
        The goal is speed, not full comprehension. Skip unfamiliar words unless they seem essential to the main idea.
        
    5. **Glance Over the Introduction and Conclusion**
        
        These sections often restate the main points in condensed form.
        
    
    ### Tactics for Scanning
    
    1. **Know What You‚Äôre Looking For**
        
        Have a clear target (e.g., a date, percentage, or name) before you begin.
        
    2. **Use Visual Clues**
        
        Numbers, capital letters, italics, and bold type stand out and can guide your eyes.
        
    3. **Move Your Eyes Quickly Down the Page**
        
        Let your eyes jump from section to section rather than reading in order.
        
    4. **Use Finger or Pen Guidance**
        
        Pointing at the page while moving downward can help you stay focused.
        
    5. **Ignore Irrelevant Information**
        
        Skip over sentences or sections that don‚Äôt contain your target.
