- Access your space in [Past experiences.docx](https://eduuaa.sharepoint.com/:w:/s/WritingI/EVFXr7lA6DRKmHpH-XPOoT4BZnOW5GKLPLhiOORfZZZ75g?e=dRgDwp).
- Brainstorm on a past experience and begin reading to find a specific fact, statistic, example, or detail related to your main idea or topic sentence.
- Write out 20 key terms related to your story, and write out each of the four tenses in the past: simple past, past perfect, past continuous, and past perfect continuous.
- Develop a topic sentence that states the past experience and the _==one==_ key concept that relates to the experience: empathy, responsibility, courage, relationships, love, forgiveness, dedication, etc.
- Based on your topic sentence, find a detail, fact, statistic, or example that you can cite according to APA.
- After your topic sentence (the first sentence of the paragraph) and the evidence sentence (the second sentence of the paragraph), complete the rest of your paragraph by including two analysis sentences and a summarizing sentence.
