# Task
- Writing prompts
    1. **My Dream Job**: Write about what job you want to have in the future. What will you do every day? Why do you want this job?
    2. **A Day in 2050**: Imagine a day in the year 2050. What will the world look like? What new technology will people use?
    3. **Future Travel**: Write about a place you want to visit in the future. How will you get there? What will you do when you arrive?
    4. **My Future Family**: Describe your future family. How many people will be in your family? What will you do together?
    5. **Life in Space**: Imagine living on another planet in the future. What will your home look like? What will you eat and do for fun?
- Choose one of the Word documents in [Week 16](https://eduuaa.sharepoint.com/:f:/s/WritingI/EuBiMmYaTdtEof40nxOgr_4Bm1bs0oZNw3BenrTXQWei3Q?e=d0pOwL) and rename it using your full name, first name first.
- Choose one of the five writing prompts above and copy and paste it to your Word document in Teams.
- Answer the question according to the following:
    - One paragraph, double spaced with a 0.5 inch indentation.
    - The average number of words per sentence should be 15.
    - Paragraph should include at least one simple, complex, and compound sentence.
    - Paragraph should have at least one relative clause and one subordinating clause.
    - Paragraph should have between 140-180 words.
    - Paragraph should include a topic sentence to begin the paragraph, details, examples, and facts to support the topic sentence, and a summarizing sentence to conclude the paragraph.
    - No citations and references are required; all details should be anecdotal and based on personal experience.
    - Paragraph must be submitted to the [Teams assignment](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/entity/66aeee93-507d-479a-a3ef-8f494af43945/classroom?context=%7B%22subEntityId%22%3A%22%7B%5C%22version%5C%22%3A%5C%221.0%5C%22%2C%5C%22config%5C%22%3A%7B%5C%22classes%5C%22%3A%5B%7B%5C%22id%5C%22%3A%5C%22bdcacce9-d67b-4e01-b7d8-e7ca2bab99fe%5C%22%2C%5C%22assignmentIds%5C%22%3A%5B%5C%2257674ac7-f3e6-4a0f-b408-b7807ac92f45%5C%22%5D%7D%5D%7D%2C%5C%22action%5C%22%3A%5C%22navigate%5C%22%2C%5C%22view%5C%22%3A%5C%22assignment-viewer%5C%22%2C%5C%22appId%5C%22%3A%5C%221fec8e78-bce4-4aaf-ab1b-5451cc387264%5C%22%2C%5C%22deeplinkType%5C%22%3A4%7D%22%2C%22channelId%22%3Anull%7D) no later than 2:00 PM, Friday, November 29, 2024.
