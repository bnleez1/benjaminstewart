> [!important] As we begin a new semester, it's important that you have an opportunity to express your expectations you have of me, your instructor, and of the course itself. Knowing your expectations from the beginning of class helps me better prepare my instruction according to your needs, wants, and personal preferences. The online form found on this page is where I'll ask that you submit your expectations, which will remain confidential. I will share group results, however, I will not share individual responses; that is, your responses will remain confidential.
## General format for answering the questionnaire

> [!important] The questions below are the types of information that you may include in the online question youâ€™ll be asked to complete.
**Course Expectations Questionnaire**
Thank you for taking the time to share your expectations for this course. Your feedback is valuable in helping me tailor the learning experience to your needs. Please answer the following questions as openly and honestly as possible.
**About You**
1. What is your preferred name? How should I refer to you?
2. What semester are you currently in (Prope, 1st, 2nd, etc.)?
**Expectations for the Instructor**
1. What qualities do you value most in an instructor (e.g., approachability, organization, enthusiasm, etc.)?
2. How do you prefer to receive feedback (e.g., written comments, individual meetings, in-class discussions)?
3. What types of support do you expect from your instructor outside of class time (e.g., office hours, online chat responses, etc.)?
4. Are there any specific teaching styles or methods that you find particularly effective?
**Expectations for the Course**
1. What are your main goals for taking this course?
2. What topics or concepts are you most interested in learning about?
3. How do you prefer to learn (e.g., lectures, discussions, hands-on activities, group work, pair work, individual work)?
4. What types of assessments do you find most helpful in measuring your learning (e.g., exams, essays, projects, presentations, etc.)?
5. Do you have any concerns or challenges that you anticipate in this course?
**Onboarding**
1. Can you sign in to the virtual classroom ([https://aulavirtual.uaa.mx/my/courses.php](https://aulavirtual.uaa.mx/my/courses.php)) and Notion ([https://www.notion.so/](https://www.notion.so/)) for your enrolled course?
2. After having looked over the [[Writing error code list]], do you have any particular concerns related to your understanding and/or skills when it comes to grammar?
3. After having read [[e-Portfolios- Empowering your career as an English language teacher trainer]], state whether you will be starting a new eportfolio, maintaining an eportfolio that you already have, or if you will not be contributing to an eportfolio this semester. Make sure you review the course syllabus to see whether or not an eportfolio is required.
**Additional Comments**
1. Is there anything else you would like me to know about your expectations or learning preferences?
**Confidentiality**
Your responses will be kept confidential. Aggregated data may be shared with the class to facilitate discussion and mutual understanding of expectations and how we can best work together.
**Thank You!**
Your feedback is greatly appreciated. I look forward to a successful and engaging semester together.
