- Microteaching scenarios
    
    ### **1) Scenario: Deconstructing Labels ‚Äì Dialects vs. Varieties**
    
    Trainees present a short dialogue or written example showcasing a language/dialect stereotype.
    
    What task(s) might you include in your 15-minute micro-teaching lesson?
    
    **Reflection Focus:**
    
    - What pedagogical risks does labeling pose?
    - How can teachers present language variation neutrally and respectfully?
    - What approaches help students notice variation without reinforcing bias?
    
    ---
    
    ### **2) Scenario: Code-Switching in the Classroom**
    
    Trainees dramatize a short classroom interaction where a multilingual student shifts between English and their heritage language (or dialect) in response to peer questions or to assist comprehension.
    
    What task(s) might you include in your 15-minute micro-teaching lesson?
    
    **Reflection Focus:**
    
    - When and how might code-switching be strategically valued in the ELT classroom?
    - How can teachers foster awareness of code-switching as a legitimate communicative resource, rather than treating it as error?
