## 2b **Didactic Sequence for Teaching Grammar (1 hour)**
- Present a comprehensive didactic sequence for teaching grammar, including planning, teaching, and assessment.
- Cover lesson planning, introduction, practice, reinforcement, and evaluation.
- Discuss strategies for addressing common learner difficulties.
**3.2 Group Activity: Creating a Lesson Plan (1 hour)**
- Divide participants into groups.
- Assign each group a grammar topic and ask them to create a lesson plan following the didactic sequence.
- Groups present their plans to the class, receiving feedback.
**Day 4: Practical Application and Practice (2 hours)**
**4.1 Teaching Grammar Practicum (2 hours)**
- Participants take turns teaching a short grammar lesson based on their lesson plans from the previous day.
- Each session is followed by peer feedback and discussion.
- The instructor provides guidance and constructive feedback.
**Day 5: Assessment and Conclusion (2 hours)**
**5.1 Assessing Grammar (1 hour)**
- Discuss various methods of assessing grammar skills.
- Explain the importance of formative and summative assessment.
- Share examples of grammar assessment tasks.
**5.2 Recap and Conclusion (1 hour)**
- Summarize key points from the entire unit.
- Invite questions and reflections from participants.
- Discuss the role of ongoing professional development in teaching grammar.
