    3. How does the immune system respond differently to bacterial infections compared to viral infections?
    4. In what ways do the symptoms of the common cold differ from those of the flu?
    5. How do the effects of stress on mental health compare to its effects on physical health?

# Assessment
- Include at least one simple, compound, and complex sentence in each sentence.
- Paragraph has a topic sentence, details, and a summary or conclusion sentence (in this order).
- Includes the question, paragraph (at least 100 words, five-to-eight sentences), and theme-rheme patterns.
- Write paragraph in the third person
- Should compare two items based on the question, using comparative adjectives.
- Paragraph is unified, coherent, and cohesive.
