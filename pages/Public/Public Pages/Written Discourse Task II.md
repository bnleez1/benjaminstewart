## Task
In your respective breakout rooms, record an online session in Teams, completing the following:
1. Assign one team member one of the written types of texts (genre): instruction leaflet, letter to/from a friend, public notice, product label, newspaper obituary, poem, news report, academic article, small ad, postcard to/from a friend, and business letter.
2. Look for the overall textual pattern of the text: problem-solution, cause-consequence, temporal sequence, instrument-achievement, contrasting something or showing equivalence of something, etc.
3. In addition to an overall textual pattern, look for word order (lexicalization), cohesion (discourse markers, transitions, etc.), and tense and aspect (changes to verb tense that link to discourse markers, etc.).
4. Review your analysis with at least one other classmate before creating an ==individual== video of your analysis (5-10 minutes long).
