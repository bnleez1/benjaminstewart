# Problem statement
## Task
- Review [[Problem Statement]] & [[Persuasive vs. Argumentative Body Paragraphs- Mastering the Art of Convincing]].
- Choose a shared Word document in [Persuasive essay](https://eduuaa.sharepoint.com/:f:/s/CAEI/EphDK2-s7x5Egxh7SqcXAqgBODFnS3-xuYpJk6z_6HGTtg?e=O2cnPk) and rename the document using your full name, first name first.
- Choose a topic for your persuasive essay (refer to the list on Tuesday in [[CAE I Week 15 lessons]].
- Review the acceptable sources of information you may use for your references (Tuesday in [[CAE I Week 15 lessons]].
- Choose a purpose and target audience and add it to your Word document.
- Based on the purpose and target audience, create a problem statement for your persuasive essay in your Word document.
- ==Copy-and-paste== your purpose, target audience, and problem statement from your Word document to the [Teams assignment](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/entity/66aeee93-507d-479a-a3ef-8f494af43945/classroom?context=%7B%22subEntityId%22%3A%22%7B%5C%22version%5C%22%3A%5C%221.0%5C%22%2C%5C%22config%5C%22%3A%7B%5C%22classes%5C%22%3A%5B%7B%5C%22id%5C%22%3A%5C%220a97527a-229c-4155-8f43-be4831abf2bf%5C%22%2C%5C%22assignmentIds%5C%22%3A%5B%5C%22161a92c4-51a5-4550-bdcc-75136bab31e6%5C%22%5D%7D%5D%7D%2C%5C%22action%5C%22%3A%5C%22navigate%5C%22%2C%5C%22view%5C%22%3A%5C%22assignment-viewer%5C%22%2C%5C%22appId%5C%22%3A%5C%221fec8e78-bce4-4aaf-ab1b-5451cc387264%5C%22%2C%5C%22deeplinkType%5C%22%3A4%7D%22%2C%22channelId%22%3Anull%7D) as a Word document.
