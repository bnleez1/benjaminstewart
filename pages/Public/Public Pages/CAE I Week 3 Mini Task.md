> [!important] In this task, you will work in your assigned teams as lifestyle consultants to design and present a daily routine for a fictional character. Your goal is to create a weekly schedule that balances the character's work, leisure, and personal care, while improving their productivity and well-being. You'll analyze the character's background, prioritize tasks, and develop a routine that meets their unique needs. Your group will present the routine, explaining your choices and using visual aids to make your case. This task will challenge your critical thinking, collaboration, and English language skills, all while helping you learn to present ideas clearly and persuasively.
### **Goal**
You will design and present a daily routine for a fictional character that integrates critical thinking skills. The goal is to analyze and prioritize daily tasks, considering the character's profession, lifestyle, and personal goals. The presentation should be engaging and provide a logical rationale for the chosen routine.
### **Role**
You will take on the role of lifestyle consultants who have been hired to optimize the daily routine of a fictional character to improve their productivity and well-being.
### **Audience**
The audience will be the fictional character (played by another group) who will assess the practicality and effectiveness of the proposed routine, offering feedback on the clarity, logic, and creativity of the presentation. Team 2 is the audience for Team 1, Team 3 is the audience for Team 2, Team 4 is the audience for Team 3, Team 5 is the audience for Team 4, Team 6 is the audience for Team 5, Team 7 is the audience for Team 6, Team 8 is the audience for Team 7, Team 9 is the audience for Team 8, Team 1 is the audience for Team 9
### **Situation**
The fictional character is facing challenges in managing time effectively, leading to stress and inefficiency. The students, as lifestyle consultants, must devise a daily routine that balances work, leisure, and personal care. The routine must be adaptable and sustainable, taking into account the character's unique needs and circumstances. Be creative when defining the character‚Äôs unique needs and circumstances, etc.
### **Performance**
In your assigned teams:
1. **Analyze the character's unhealthy background**: Determine the character‚Äôs profession, lifestyle, and personal goals provided. Feel free to use your imagination and/or AI to generate ideas of unhealthy lifestyles, profession, etc.
2. **Prioritize daily tasks**: Identify which tasks are essential for the character's success and well-being, and which can be minimized or eliminated.
3. **Design a daily routine**: Create a detailed schedule that reflects the character's priorities, including time for work, leisure, personal care, and any other relevant activities.
4. **Prepare a presentation**: Develop a clear and persuasive presentation that explains the rationale behind the chosen routine. The presentation should include visual aids (e.g., a timetable, charts) and anticipate potential objections or challenges.
5. **Deliver the presentation**: Present the daily routine to the character and classmates, defending the choices made and demonstrating how the routine meets the character's needs.
### **Standards**
- **Critical Thinking**: Demonstrate the ability to analyze, evaluate, and prioritize information relevant to the character's daily routine.
- **Communication**: Present ideas clearly and persuasively, using appropriate language and visual aids.
- **Collaboration**: Work effectively as a team, contributing to the planning and presentation of the routine.
- **Creativity**: Offer innovative solutions to the character's time management challenges.
- **Language Proficiency**: Use appropriate grammar, vocabulary, and sentence structures at a B1-B2 level, with a focus on clarity and fluency in both written and spoken English: time expressions, adverbs of frequency, etc.
