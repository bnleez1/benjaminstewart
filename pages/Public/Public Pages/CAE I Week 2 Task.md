# Game Day Friday!

> [!important] Play a game to practice discussing hobbies.
## Task
1. Play a game for 50 minutes (9:00 AM - 10:00 AM) and record your conversations spoken entirely in English.
2. A typical 50-minute conversation should include around 4,250 words, or 1,062 words per minute (total words=minutes x 85). Your goal is to speak at least 3,200 in English as a group of four or 800 words per person or 2,400 words or 800 words per person for a group of three. A group of two should reach 4,250 words between the two of you.
3. Groups should be between three to four.
4. Have at least two members of your group record your conversations (audio only), making sure phones are located close to the speakers to get a better recording.
5. Use Word online to transcribe automatically the audio to text. Refer to [[Transcribe audio to text]].
6. Determine the following: total words spoken in English among the entire group, total words per person spoken in English.
7. The audio and transcription should be uploaded to [Game Day Audios & Transcription](https://eduuaa.sharepoint.com/:f:/s/CAEI2025/EvUr5AdsEpVPiFT07-Bnq5ABDDU3v6JCOm1IfZIIfBWJ8w?e=TldXf0) in Teams. Name both files identically using only your first names of each member who participated.
