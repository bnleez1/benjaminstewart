_Languages_ is an international, multidisciplinary, **[peer-reviewed](https://www.mdpi.com/editorial_process)**, open access journal on interdisciplinary studies of languages published monthly online by MDPI. **[The European Society for Transcultural and Interdisciplinary Dialogue (ESTIDIA)](https://www.estidia.eu/)** is affiliated with _Languages_ and its members receive discounts on the article processing charges.
- **[Open Access](https://www.mdpi.com/openaccess)**— free for readers, with **[article processing charges (APC)](https://www.mdpi.com/journal/languages/apc)** paid by authors or their institutions.
- **High Visibility:** indexed within **[Scopus](https://www.scopus.com/sourceid/21101020136)**, **[ESCI (Web of Science)](https://mjl.clarivate.com/search-results?issn=2226-471X&hide_exact_match_fl=true&utm_source=mjl&utm_medium=share-by-link&utm_campaign=search-results-share-this-journal)**, **[ERIH Plus](https://dbh.nsd.uib.no/publiseringskanaler/erihplus/)**, and **[other databases](https://www.mdpi.com/journal/languages/indexing)**.
- **Journal Rank:** JCR - Q2 (_Linguistics_) / CiteScore - Q1 (Language and Linguistics)
- **Rapid Publication:** manuscripts are peer-reviewed and a first decision is provided to authors approximately 49.6 days after submission; acceptance to publication is undertaken in 10.6 days (median values for papers published in this journal in the first half of 2024).
- **Recognition of Reviewers:** reviewers who provide timely, thorough peer-review reports receive vouchers entitling them to a discount on the APC of their next publication in any MDPI journal, in appreciation of the work done.
