## MEAL Plan Synopsis (body paragraph)
When writing a thesis paper, there are three main types of paragraphs: introductory, body, and conclusion paragraphs. An introductory paragraph sets the stage with a thesis statement, while body paragraphs develop the main ideas with evidence and analysis. Conclusion paragraphs summarize the thesis and main points. In a five-paragraph essay, these types are used, except for transitional paragraphs, which are specific to longer papers. Each paragraph type plays a crucial role in structuring the thesis and ensuring clarity and coherence in the argument.
The MEAL plan (Main idea, Evidence, Analysis, Linking) is a structured approach to writing effective body paragraphs. It begins with a clear topic sentence that introduces the main idea, followed by evidence sentences that provide factual support. Analysis sentences interpret the evidence, explaining its relevance to the main idea. Finally, linking sentences transition to the next paragraph or summarize the current one. This method ensures that each paragraph is cohesive and contributes to the overall argument of the thesis.

## Topic sentences rewritten

> [!important] It‚Äôs usually best to create topic sentences that focus more on a possible solution than the problem itself. Always check with your instructor when making this distinction.
- Topic sentence 1: Research has consistently shown that the meat industry has a significant environmental impact.
    - **Rewritten**: The meat industry impacts the environment significantly when...
- Topic sentence 2: One of the main ways that meat production harms the environment is by contributing to greenhouse gas emissions and climate change.
    - **Rewritten**: Meat production contributes to greenhouse gas emissions and climate change, which harms the environment in a variety of ways.
- Topic sentence 3: Another major environmental problem caused by the meat industry is water pollution and depletion.
    - **Rewritten**: The meat industry causes water pollution and depletion, which is another major environmental problem.
- Topic sentence 4: The meat industry also affects the environment by causing deforestation and biodiversity loss.
    - **Rewritten**: Deforestation and biodiversity loss result from the meat industry that affects the environment as well.
- Topic sentence 5: Besides the environmental consequences, the meat industry also has negative effects on human health and animal welfare.
    - **Rewritten**: The meat industry not only affects human health and animal welfare but also results in environmental consequences.
- Topic sentence 6: Despite the overwhelming evidence of the environmental and ethical costs of meat consumption, global demand for meat is still rising.
    - **Rewritten**: Global demand for meat still rises still despite the overwhelming evidence of the environmental and ethical costs of meat consumption.
- Topic sentence 7: To address this challenge, various solutions have been proposed, such as reducing meat consumption, adopting plant-based diets, or switching to alternative sources of protein.
    - **Rewritten**: Reducing meat consumption and adopting plant-based diets offer a solution to harming the planet.
- Topic sentence 8: Reducing meat consumption is the most effective and feasible way to lower the environmental impact of the food system.
    - **Rewritten**: The environmental impact of the food system directly links to how effective and feasible it is to reduce meat consumption.
- Topic sentence 9: Plant-based diets offer many benefits for the environment, as well as for human health and animal welfare.
    - **Rewritten**: Many benefits for the environment, human health, and animal welfare come from plant-based diets.
- Topic sentence 10: Alternative sources of protein, such as insects, lab-grown meat, or cultured meat, are promising innovations, but they also face some challenges and limitations.
    - **Rewritten**: Promising innovations like lab-grown meat and cultured mean offer alternatives for those who enjoy eating meat notwithstanding their challenges and limitations.
