## Task
- Active-passive voice activity (from Monday).
- Reported speech activity (from Tuesday).
- Writing prompt (in class)
    
    > [!important] Choose one of the following prompts and create one paragraph with 150-200 words, 5-8 sentences with a main idea, supporting sentences, and a conclusion sentence.
    
    1. **Prompt**: Describe a conversation you had with a friend about a recent movie you watched. Include both reported speech and active and passive voice.
    2. **Prompt**: Write about a news story you heard on the radio. Use reported speech to convey the information and mix active and passive voice.
    3. **Prompt**: Describe a situation where you had to explain a complex topic to someone. Use reported speech and mix active and passive voice.
    4. **Prompt**: Write about a time when you received advice from a family member. Include reported speech and both active and passive voice.
    5. **Prompt**: Narrate a conversation you had with a friend about their travel experiences. Use reported speech and mix active and passive voice.
    6. **Prompt**: Describe a time when you had to give a presentation. Use reported speech and both active and passive voice.
    7. **Prompt**: Write about a book you recently read and discussed with someone. Use reported speech and mix active and passive voice.
- Submit active-passive voice and reported speech activities to [Teams assignment](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/entity/66aeee93-507d-479a-a3ef-8f494af43945/classroom?context=%7B%22subEntityId%22%3A%22%7B%5C%22version%5C%22%3A%5C%221.0%5C%22%2C%5C%22config%5C%22%3A%7B%5C%22classes%5C%22%3A%5B%7B%5C%22id%5C%22%3A%5C%220a97527a-229c-4155-8f43-be4831abf2bf%5C%22%2C%5C%22assignmentIds%5C%22%3A%5B%5C%22c36afaff-1667-48c7-b1d3-7978e4568ca4%5C%22%5D%7D%5D%7D%2C%5C%22action%5C%22%3A%5C%22navigate%5C%22%2C%5C%22view%5C%22%3A%5C%22assignment-viewer%5C%22%2C%5C%22appId%5C%22%3A%5C%221fec8e78-bce4-4aaf-ab1b-5451cc387264%5C%22%2C%5C%22deeplinkType%5C%22%3A4%7D%22%2C%22channelId%22%3Anull%7D) (==during class==).
## Assessment
- [ ] Active-passive voice activity completed in Teams (week 10). Entire text should be analyzed, looking for subjects (noun phrases), verb phrases (and any adverbs), and distinguishing between active and passive voice.
- [ ] Reported speech activity completed in Teams (week 10). Only the excerpt you converted to reported speech - Include both the direct quotes and the reported speech.
- [ ] Paragraph written in class with no devices used (handwritten notes permitted).
- [ ] Paragraph includes main idea, supporting sentences, and conclusion.
- [ ] Standard English used: grammar, vocabulary, punctuation, capitalization, etc.
