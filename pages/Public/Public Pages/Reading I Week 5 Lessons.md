> [!important] **Enduring understanding**: The purpose of this module is to develop your reading skills by exploring texts about university life. This is a world full of new schedules, people, and academic challenges. By understanding texts on this topic, you will build the vocabulary and strategic reading skills necessary to succeed in your own academic journey and feel more confident in an English-speaking university environment.

> [!important] **Essential Question**: How does university life challenge us to manage our time, develop new skills, and shape our future?

> [!important] **Reading**: [[4) The University Experience]]

> [!important] **Learning outcomes**
> 
> :
> 
> - Obtain general and specific information from texts about the university experience.
> - Identify the main ideas in paragraphs about student life and academic responsibilities.
> - Infer the meaning of unfamiliar academic vocabulary by using contextual clues.
> - Recognize and understand key vocabulary and grammar used to discuss schedules, classes, and campus life.
