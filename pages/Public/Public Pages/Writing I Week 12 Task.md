# Assessment Criteria
- Paragraph includes a parenthetical citation that does not mention any authors or studies (but includes a citation at the end of the sentence with the author‚Äôs name and year of publication).
- Includes a reference in the reference page at the end of the document, according to APA and alphabetized.
- Include at least one simple, compound, and complex sentence in each sentence.
- Paragraph has a topic sentence, details, and a summary or conclusion sentence (in this order).
- Paragraph includes 130-140 words with five-to-eight sentences.
- Paragraph is unified (on topic), coherent (organized), and cohesive (smooth transition of ideas from one sentence to the next).
